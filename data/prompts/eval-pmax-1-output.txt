## Outcome digest

**What it does**
Reads the latest bug-tracker CSV export, compares it to the prior week's export, and emits a Markdown status report with headline counts, top regressions, stalled tickets, and closed-this-week tickets.

**Key inputs**
Current and prior weekly CSV exports in the exports/ folder matching bugs_*.csv.

**Done when**
Every open bug in the current export appears in exactly one section and the counts reconcile with raw row totals.

**Quick sample**

    ## Headline counts
    Open: 47 (+3 from last week)
    Closed this week: 12
    Stalled (>14 days no update): 8

    ## Top regressions
    BUG-1204  login retry loop after password reset
    BUG-1198  export CSV truncates unicode titles
