## Outcome digest

**What it does**
Turns a list of merged pull requests into a CHANGELOG.md section grouped by Added, Changed, Fixed, and Removed, with each author credited by handle.

**Key inputs**
The supplied list of merged PRs and the existing CHANGELOG.md with its last release tag.

**Done when**
Every supplied PR appears exactly once under one of the four group headings under a new version heading.
